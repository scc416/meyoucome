Why recycled paper tissue?

Producing a ton of recycled paper is more environmentally friendly than virgin paper.

  • Protect 24 trees from being logged
  • Use 49% water less
  • Use 33% electricity less
  • Reduce 37% of carbon release
  • Reduce 39% solid waste
Mil Mill Recycled Tissue is an example of circular economy. By turning waste paper into recycled products, we make use of existing materials to supply daily necessities to ourselves, which also conduct to sustainable living.
